Privacy Policy

RealLife Focus  ·  Last updated: 6 March 2026

⚡ Short version

RealLife Focus works entirely on your device. We collect no personal data, transmit nothing to external servers, and have no analytics, advertising, or accounts. Your data never leaves your phone.

📦 Data we collect None

RealLife Focus does not collect, store, share, or sell any personal information. The app operates fully offline and makes no network requests.

Everything the app stores lives exclusively on your device:

  • Block profiles and schedules you create
  • The list of apps you choose to block
  • Your local override-attempt history (audit log)
  • App preferences (e.g. override duration)

This data is stored in a local SQLite database and Android SharedPreferences. It is never uploaded anywhere.

🔑 Android permissions & why we need them

  • Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS) — detects which app is in the foreground so the blocker can display the lock screen when a blocked app is opened. Data is read locally; never stored beyond the current poll cycle.
  • Draw Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) — shows the lock-screen overlay above other apps.
  • Accessibility Service (BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE) — optional fallback on devices (e.g. Xiaomi, Huawei) that restrict background usage-stats access. Only foreground app-change events are read; no keystrokes, text, or content is captured.
  • Foreground Service / Battery Optimization Exemption — keeps the blocking service running reliably in the background.
  • Receive Boot Completed — restarts the service automatically after a reboot.
  • Post Notifications — shows the persistent service notification and override-countdown timer required by Android 8+.
  • Query All Packages — enumerates installed apps so you can choose which ones to block. The list stays on-device.

🔗 Third-party services None

The app contains no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash-reporting services, or social login. The only libraries used are official AndroidX / Google Jetpack libraries from the Android Open Source Project.

👶 Children's privacy

RealLife Focus does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is appropriate for all ages.

📝 Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date. Because we hold no contact information, we cannot notify you directly — please check back if you have questions.

Given that the app collects no data, material changes that could negatively affect users are not anticipated.
